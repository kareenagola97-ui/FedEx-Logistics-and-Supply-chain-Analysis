# FedEx Logistics and Supply Chain Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes FedEx logistics and supply chain data to identify shipment trends, vendor performance, freight cost patterns, and product demand. The analysis aims to generate actionable business insights that can help improve logistics efficiency and reduce operational costs.

## Problem Statement
FedEx manages shipments across multiple countries, vendors, products, and transportation modes. Understanding shipment patterns, freight costs, and vendor performance is essential for optimizing supply chain operations and improving business efficiency.

## Objectives
- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
- Analyze shipment trends and logistics performance.
- Identify top countries, vendors, products, and brands.
- Evaluate freight cost and shipment weight relationships.
- Generate business insights and recommendations.

## Key Insights
- Air shipment mode is used most frequently and incurs the highest freight cost.
- A few vendors handle the majority of shipments.
- Freight cost increases with shipment weight.
- Some countries contribute significantly to shipment volume and value.
- A small number of products and brands drive most shipments.

## Business Recommendations
- Optimize shipment mode selection to reduce costs.
- Strengthen relationships with top-performing vendors.
- Improve inventory planning for high-demand products.
- Focus logistics resources on high-volume countries.
- Use data-driven forecasting for better supply chain planning.

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates end-to-end data analysis, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, visualization, and business insight generation for logistics and supply chain operations.
